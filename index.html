Micheline Verdant – Stories That Heal, Uplift, and Resonate

One story brings the warmth of the islands and the wisdom of a mother’s love. The other cracks open the soul with poetry that bleeds truth and healing. Together, they speak to every version of you—young, hurting, growing.

Kaila and the Beach

This isn’t just a day at the beach — it’s a day full of lessons. Through a young girl’s innocent eyes, Micheline captures how a twist in the weather can shift your world, and how love and perspective can brighten even the darkest skies. It’s warm, fun, culturally rich, and the kind of story kids will carry with them long after the last page.

Sickness & Separation

These poems don’t whisper — they speak. They echo the deep stuff — heartbreak, healing, sickness, soul-searching. You don’t just read these lines. You feel them. Every poem is a mirror, a letter, a prayer. This collection is for anyone who's walked through emotional fire and is still standing.

What Keeps You Turn The Pages?

It’s the honesty. The heart. The way every word feels like it was written just for you. Whether it’s a playful beach day that turns into a life lesson or poetry that cuts deep into the soul, Michelines storytelling pulls you in. Her characters feel real, her emotions unfiltered. There’s a rhythm to her writing — a gentle flow that carries you from one line to the next. You’ll laugh, reflect, maybe even cry. But more than anything, you’ll keep reading because something in these pages feels like home.
